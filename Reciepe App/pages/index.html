Grandma's secret Apple Pie

This apple pie reciepe has been in my family for generations

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 sugar cup
  2. 2 tsp ground cinanmon
  3. 6 medium sized apples, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375 F (190 C)
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinanmon
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinamon mixture
